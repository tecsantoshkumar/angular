What is Multimedia?

Multimedia is the computer-assisted blending of text, drawings, stills, moving images (videos), 3d animation, graphic design, audio, video animation, and any other media in which all the types of interactions can be expressed, stored, communicated, and processed digitally.

Multimedia has the components of text, pictures, audio, and video with links and tools that allow the information for users to be used to navigate, engage, create, and communicate digitally

Multimedia and design are closely linked to many other disciplines. These specializations include the latest 3d Animation, Photography, Visual Effects as well as graphic design, Writing, Digital Art, Intellectual Property, and Media Arts.
