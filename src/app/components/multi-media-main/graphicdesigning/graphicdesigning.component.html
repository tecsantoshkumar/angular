What is Graphic Designing?

Graphic designing is generally defined as the art of planning and delivering ideas and experiences professionally with visual and textual content. In other terms, in graphic design communicates ideas or messages in a visual way. These designs can be a simple business logo or complex as page layouts on a website. Graphic design takes graphical and textual elements and implements them into multiple types of media as it conveys the visual messages or ideas of the project, event, campaign, or product. Graphic design is the craft and practice of composing and arranging the visual elements professionally for the project. Commercial advertisements, designing logos for a website, Designing the layout of a magazine, creating a poster for a theatre performance, and designing packaging for a product are all examples of graphic design

Graphic designs are used by many companies to promote and sell their products through advertising, websites will convey complicated information in a digestible way through infographics, businesses will develop their identity through branding, among other things.
