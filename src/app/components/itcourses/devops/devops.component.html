What is DevOps?

The word DevOps says that it is a combination of two words that is Development and Operations. It is a software development technique that provides the collaboration between Software Development and Information Technology Operations to make the software production and deployment in an automated & repeatable way. DevOps is preferred as high-quality software in very short development cycles & helps to increase the organization’s speed to deliver software applications and services which ends up in greater user satisfaction.

DevOps, the IT Operations team is completely aware of the project that the developers are making. These IT Operations teams interact with developers and jointly develop a monitoring plan that caters to the delivery of the IT business needs. They also use advanced Application Performance Monitoring (APM) automated Tools. DevOps merge the development and operation team for faster, smoother, and timely delivery of product or application. Without DevOps, developers are spending a large amount of their time in testing, deploying, and designing for building the project. DevOps has a deep integration between the process of development and operations. Learning DevOps is impossible without having in-depth knowledge of DevOps lifecycle.
