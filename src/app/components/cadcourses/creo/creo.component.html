Introduction to CREO Parametric.

CREO is a Software suite of programs that were developed by Parametric Technology Corporation (PTC) to assist in the design of products. In the past, Creo was called Pro/Engineer (Pro E). It is a CAD/CAM/CAE-based 3D solid modeling software. Creo Parametric is utilized by mechanical engineers/designers to perform Solid modeling and assembly modeling Finite element analysis two-dimensional orthographic viewing sub-divisional and NURBS surfacing as well as parametric and direct modeling, as well as NC and tooling functions.

Creo Elements/Parametric competes with other programs like SolidWorks, CATIA, and NX/Solid Edge. Creo parametric can be described as an updated version of Pro-E that is ideal for analysis, modeling, and simulation. It is widely employed for design and development at various automobile companies and manufacturing industries.
