What is ETABS?

The ETABS is known as Extended Three-Dimensional Analysis of Building System. It is a highly sophisticated and useful special-purpose analysis and design program specifically designed to work with construction systems. Massive and complex models of buildings are easily adapted by integrating systems. The ETABS modeling is the notion that multi-story structures typically contain similar or identical floor plans that are repeated on a vertical basis.

Advantages of ETABS Software:

  • ETABS is the most powerful tool that has visual and object-based interfaces that allow drawing CAD-like drawings.

  • It enhances the productivity of structural engineers in the construction industry.

  • It saves time and is greater efficient than general-purpose programs.

The main Features of ETABS Software

  • Building Modeling features that facilitate the analytical-model generation, as well as simulate advanced seismic systems are described in the following order:

  • Templates for the global system and local element modeling in the analysis design of the course.

  • Custom-designed Section geometry and characteristic behavior

  • The grouping of frames and Shell objects

  • Link assignment to model dampers, isolators, and other advanced seismic systems

  • Nonlinear hinge specification

  • Automated connection using manual choices

  • Assignment and editing features for elevation, plan, and 3D views
