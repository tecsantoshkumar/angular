Key Topics covered in CAD Training.

Civil Courses:

AutoCAD civil: AutoCAD is the most popular course for Autodesk software for designing and drafting to create precise 2D drawings and 3D models. students will learn the skills of building planning and designs, 3D modeling, and drawing software. 

Civil 3d: AutoCAD Civil 3D software is used in civil engineering which enables building information modeling (BIM) workflows. students will learn the skills about designing to make you understand the surfaces, lay out parcels, and design geometry by including the making of horizontal alignments and vertical profiles, and many more.

ETABS: ETABS is referred to as Extended 3D Analysis of Building Systems. ETABS is the most popular Engineering software application that is widely used for multi-storied building structural analysis as well as structural design.  that students can get a complete knowledge of analysis of building structures.

Sketch up: Sketch Up is the most popular tool to create 3D models in architecture, interior design, woodworking, or engineering. students will learn the skills of creating and editing 2D and 3D models with a patented “Push/Pull'' technology. 

Mx road: With Mx road, designers create rapid & accurate designs of all types of road works. It is widely used by civil engineers, designers, geotechnical engineers, professional surveyors, and stormwater/drainage/sewer systems. students will learn the skills of 3D modeling, construction-driven engineering works, and all types of analysis using a single application. system.

Staad pro: Structural Analysis and Design Software simplify the BIM workflow by automatically converting a physical model into an analytical model. students will learn about how to analyze civil engineering structures such as steel, concrete, timber, aluminum, and cold-formed steel designs. 

3ds max: Autodesk 3DS Max is the most popular course and it is the most efficient software for modeling and rendering tools. Autodesk 3DS Max is widely used in architecture, manufacturing, industrial design, and many more like visual graphics models. The student will learn about this software, from modeling and texturing to lighting and rendering. 

Revit Architecture: Autodesk Revit is an industry-standard tool designed for building information modeling or BIM. The student will be able to learn floor plans, elevations, sections, and 3D designing. 

Mechanical Courses:

AutoCAD (Mechanical): AutoCAD is the most popular course it is software for designing and drafting and it is widely used to create precise 2D drawings and 3D models. The student will learn about mechanical designs and the manufacturing of machinery parts. 

Unigraphics: Unigraphics is the most popular application software in which 3D modeling can be done. It offers CAD/CAM/CAE in its package. The student will learn about designing, analyzing, and manufacturing purposes.

Solid works: SOLIDWORKS is one of the most significant and powerful 3D CAD programs. The student will learn 2D and 3D parametric modelers, and drafting systems. 

3D printing: 3D Printing is a technique of transforming CAD files into 3D objects with the help of a 3D printer. The student will learn additive manufacturing, working with 3d printing, how to print 3D on a physical 3D printer, and much more.

ANSYS: ANSYS Workbench is a widely used and most popular engineering analysis tool used for Finite Element Analysis (FEA), students will learn FE analysis for all types of machine tools and structures. 

CNC: CNC (Computer Numerical Control) Programming is widely used for creating computer program instructions to control machine tools. The student will be able to learn CNC programming, G-code and M-code, offset settings, and many more.

CREO: CREO is a powerful software that handles every aspect of product design including product development, designing, and analysis. The student will learn parametric solid modeling, 3D direct modeling, and 2D orthographic views.

CATIA: CATIA refers to Computer-Aided 3D Interactive Application is one of the most prominent students will be able to learn to design, analyze, and engineer insights.
