Introduction to CNC Machine Training:

CNC is also known as computing numerical control. It is a flexible system that lets you regulate the movement of components and tools through programs in computers that utilize numerical data. CNC can be utilized on nearly every traditional machine. The most popular CNC machines in machine shops theory include CNC machines centers (CNC mill) as well as turning centers (CNC lathe).

 Motion control devices that can be automated include three major elements:

  •  Command function.
  •  A drive/motion device.
  •  A feedback system.
