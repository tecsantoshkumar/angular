What is Hardware and Networking?

IT hardware and networking are growing rapidly with time. The hardware and networking have carried ahead from physical server stations to cloud storage and virtualization. Hardware and networking are interlinks of several computer parts and networks. This interconnection can be either on a large scale basis or on a small scale basis. When you connect a very huge set of systems with each other and with the database and servers then it is large-scale interconnection. When you connect computers with scanners, printers, modems, routers, etc., it becomes small-scale interconnection.

Computer Hardware is the combination of tools, machines, and other durable equipment like physical parts of the computer system. It includes a monitor, keyboard, mouse, hard disk drive, graphic card, RAM, motherboard, etc.

Networking is the field of computer science that allows systems to exchange data or information. Information can be interchanged either by connecting through cables or wireless media.
