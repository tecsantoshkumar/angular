Introduction to 3ds MAX

3Ds Max is a popular software designed for Engineers/Architects that offers a full range of 3d animation, modeling rendering, and compositing options to a wide range of industries such as Engineering or Architectural design motion graphics, game design, editing films, and many more.

It is developed by Autodesk Media and Entertainment. It's also known as 3D Studio and 3D Studio Max. It is a highly-specialized 3D computer graphics program that is exclusively used to create 3D models animations, games, and pictures.

It is efficient in modeling and it also has an adaptable plug-in design and can be used with the Microsoft Windows platform. Typically, it is utilized by video game developers as well as commercial studios on TV. However, architects can use it to create 3D visualization of the design.

Key points in 3Ds Max Course

  • 3ds Max is widely utilized by civil engineers as well as architects to create 3D visualization of the design. It allows the designers to render their designs in realistic images that are drawn with AutoCAD or other CAD software.

  • This CAD software offers rendering options, however, it comes with additional features for controlling rendering settings. Thus, nowadays, many architects and engineers utilize CAD applications and BIM programs to model and 3d Max software to render.

  • It has the features that you require to build extremely realistic models of products.
