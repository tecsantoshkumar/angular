What is Desktop Publishing (DTP)?

DTP (Desktop Publishing) is a publishing software used to create high-quality, printed materials such as brochures, business cards, greeting cards, web pages, posters, for individuals, businesses and organizations. The DTP software provides good control over the layout of a page and design as compared to the word processor. The DTP is of two types they are Creates electronic pages, Creates virtual pages. These virtual pages are used to publish high-quality printed materials on physical papers such as magazines, books, newspapers, etc.

Desktop publishing (DTP) software is designed for creating visual communications for many small businesses and organizations and they choose designs depending on their capabilities, budget, and personal preference. DTP is used to publish quality, professional-looking informational documents, and books for content. This DTP software includes Adobe InDesign, Adobe FrameMaker, Microsoft Publisher, and QuarkXPress.
