What is CATIA V5?

CATIA can be described as a platform-independent software suite that supports CAD (Computer-Aided Design) as well as the CAM (Computer-Aided Manufacturing) and CAE (Computer-Aided Engineering), PLM (Product Lifecycle Management), and 3D design created through Dassault Systemes, a French Company.

Dassault Systemes released CATIA V5 which is one of the most popular parametric solid modeling tools. This course will introduce methods of solid modeling that can improve the efficiency and productivity of the end-user.

 The CATIA course is organized as an academic sequence including the Part design, Wireframe, Drafting, Surfaces, Assembly design, and Workbenches for generative Sheetmetal Designs from CATIA V5. Each session is accompanied by a thorough description of the commands and tools that CATIA V5 offers. This allows users to comprehend and utilize the program in an efficient way.

Why learn CATIA Course?

CATIA allows users to design shapes and reach a higher level of complexity of surfaces through its 3D drawing and visualizing capabilities. With CATIA software, designers can adopt an integrated system engineering approach. They can perform requirements engineering, system design, detailed simulation, and modeling to control the complicated process of development.

It facilitates the rapid development of high-quality mechanical items. Engineers can perform digital prototyping, analysis, and quality and performance simulations for any product design prior to its product development phase.
