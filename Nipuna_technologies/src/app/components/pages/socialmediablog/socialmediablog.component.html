Top 10 best ways to increase social media engagement

Social media engagement helps you strengthen your customer relationships. Customers who engage with you on social media or send you messages, you have the opportunity to build a relationship and get to know them. Social media engagement provides the opportunity to develop customer loyalty. You can increase customer loyalty by helping customers in times of need, building relationships with them, and providing a unique customer experience. Your customers will trust your brand as if you answer their questions.

You can increase your social media engagement on your pages, then you need to increase your followers for your page. You will receive more engagement on your page if you have more followers.

Social media engagement is measures the public shares, likes, and comments on an online business social networking efforts. While engagement has been used to evaluate social media performance, it doesn't always translate into sales.

Online and offline businesses need to have a Facebook presence. With more than 1.5 billion monthly users, it has become an essential part of their business. However, social media marketing is not a return-on-investment strategy. Engagement is a broad metric and doesn't reflect how many users actually buy.

How to increase social media engagement:

Facebook, Instagram, Twitter are the three most used social media platforms today. Each platform offers its own mechanism for users to show appreciation for specific posts or posters. These mechanisms are different across platforms.

  • Twitter: Retweets, followers
  • Facebook: Likes, Shares, and Followers 
  • Instagram: Likes, Followers

How popular a post is depends on Likes and shares of a post, but followings indicate that users will want to see more of your content regularly.

Create stunning images

It's the age for the visual web. Visual content is today the most popular type of content online. Research shows that images can increase interaction on Facebook by 85% and retweets on Twitter by 35%, respectively.

Your images should be striking, attractive, or tell a story but not be boring. They must also give your audience an incentive to scroll down and interact with them. Clickz.com offers some great tips for visuals that will increase your social media engagement.

Create engaging videos

Videos can help increase brand awareness, connect with customers and potential customers, and increase conversions. Develop high quality videos. This is the only way you can attract more viewers. 

Below are the five mentioned tips. These ideas will make your videos and brand stand out from others. 

  • Make killer video titles and descriptions.
  • Choose Thumbnails that Mesmerise
  • Select the video type that best suits your niche.
  • What makes engaging video content?
  • Flexibility is key to your video strategy.

Create interactive content

Interactive content requires active engagement from your audience. This includes exploring scenarios, answering questions and clicking. Interactive content is a combination of relevant and educational content and visual, entertaining and dynamic content.

  • Increase awareness
  • Improve performance
  • Your brand should be different.

Create a Facebook group

Facebook groups have a higher visibility in the newsfeed that Facebook pages. Any member of a group can post to the group and it will be visible in all members' news feeds.

Groups are great for communities and causes that allow everyone to participate in the discussion. A group can be more engaging than a Facebook page if you are able to create an appealing community or cause that will appeal to your target audience.

Take Advantage of trending topics.

For ideas on how you can attract new customers and get the conversation started, it's a smart idea to check out trending topics on Facebook. But you should not jump on the most talked about topics if they don't relate to your business.

The trending topics have changed the way people access content. They are the most talked about topics on any platform. They are a way to see what is hot and they can be adjusted to suit your location. You should start looking at trending topics to find inspiration for your next social-media conversation.

Brands can capitalize on trending topics via Facebook and Twitter. Although you only have a short window to make such content relevant, topics can be extremely popular. This classic tweet by Oreo a few years ago, which received nearly 15,000 Retweets, was a good example.

 Post at optimal times

Certain times in the day are when social media posts get the most engagement. To schedule the best times for your content, use a scheduler such as Hootsuite or Buffer App.

Quick sprout has a nice infographic that shows the best times to post on various networks. It's better to test the times that work for you and your audience before blindly following these timings.

Post Fridays

According to research published in the Social Intelligence Report, Fridays are the best days for comments, likes, and shares.

  • 17% of all comments are made on Fridays.
  •  Fridays are the day that 16% of all likes take place.
  • Fridays account for 16% of shares.
  • Sunday is the most unlikely day to get a comment on a blog post

The Social Media Examiner says that these findings are consistent to Facebook data from several years back, which shows that Facebook's Happiness Index spikes on Fridays." It might be wise to not ignore weekends when scheduling content.

Use hashtags

Hashtags were first introduced to Twitter in 2012 and have since spread to Facebook, Instagram, and Google+. This article explains how hashtags make it easy for anyone to find your content, even if they don't follow you. Hashtags get twice the social media engagement as tweets without them, and 55 percent more likely are to be retweeted.

Twitter engagement drops if you use more hashtags than 2. It's best not to use more than two hashtags on any social network, except Instagram, where more is better.

 Insert a CTA in the Post.

CTA stands for call-to-action. It is a direct instruction to tell your audience what you want. A CTA is essential to increasing your ROI if you run an ad. Even if you don't post regular content, you can increase engagement by asking your followers to respond using one of these CTAs:

  • Continue reading or Read More.
  • Click here
  • Register now
  • Please RT
  • Please share
  • Comment
  • Post a caption
  • Buy Now
  • Subscribe Now.

Your posts should be kept to the maximum length

A post of a certain length is more likely to be liked, shared and retweeted. This is the ideal length of content for social media networks.

  • Blogs: 1600 words.
  • Tweets: 71 to 100 characters.
  • Hashtag: 6 characters.
  • Facebook posts: 40 characters.
  • LinkedIn Posts: 25 words.
  • Google+ posts: 60 characters.
  • Title tags: 55 characters.
